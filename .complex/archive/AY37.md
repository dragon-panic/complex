Block edges on a parent node don't propagate to children.

`cx block H4WS V77a` creates the edge, but V77a's children (JEfl, SUnj, etc.)
still show as `ready` with `"blockers": []`. Workers and managers can claim
and work on them even though V77a itself is blocked.

Expected: if a parent is blocked, its children should not be claimable.
At minimum, `cx list --state ready` should exclude children of blocked nodes.
